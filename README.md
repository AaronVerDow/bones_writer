# Bones Writer

Bones Writer is a writer designed to encourage and train writers to flow. Every keypress is permenant and nothing can be edited, much like journaling with a pen and paper.  It was inspired by "Writing Down the Bones" by Person.

After a session is complete stats will be calculated for tracking your progress.

Features:
* No ability to make any edits
* Files are automatically saved
* Track your stats:
  * Total words
  * % mispelled words
  * % grammar errors per sentance
  * Time per session
  * Words per minute
* Autotomatically blank screen if thinking too long
